Red Fox

Foxes have a very cute, bushy tail. Even though they are adorable, they should not be taken in as housepets.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average Weight: 4.1kg to 8.7kg
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years
  • Habitat: The whole freaking world

The red fox (Vulpes vulpes), largest of the true foxes, has the greatest geographic range of all members of the Carnivora order. They are also very cute.
